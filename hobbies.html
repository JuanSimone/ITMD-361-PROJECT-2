Reading

There are a couple of books I enjoy, here as 2 of the ones I enjoyed the most:

Lord of the Mysteries

Lord of Mysteries is a western fantasy (xuanhuan) web novel written by the Chinese author "Cuttlefish That Loves Diving" (his pen name) and published on QiDian.

It is a fantasy, magical and technologically regressed world, where the main character discoveres many secretes of this world and tries to avoid destruction alongside it.

The second part has been released and I am currently waiting for it to be completed to continue reading it.

Reverend Insanity

Reverend Insanity was written by the author Gu Zhen Ren beginning around July 2017, as this was the time he released the first chapter.

The story revolves around a young man called Fang Yuan, where he is at deaths door after creating an object that will allow him to go back in time, however he has no clue of the implications that such as action will have on his life, and consequently on his fate.

Cooking

I enjoying cooking a lot recently, specially sweets. I wish to share one such dessert I have made recently.

Pudding

This is the pudding I made. I do not wish to gatekeep the recipe so this is exactly what I used in case that anyone wishes to make it:

  • 450ml milk
  • 1 Condensed Milk (1 can)
  • 2 "full" eggs
  • 4 egg yolks

And for the sweet syrup on top:

  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of Water
